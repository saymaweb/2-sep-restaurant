      Burger history

      The modern hamburger evolved in the U.S. in the early 1900s from the "Hamburg steak" brought by German immigrants. By placing minced beef patties between bread, the burger became an iconic fast food.

      Steak history

      Steak originated from ancient times when humans consumed roasted red meat, with its modern form developing in Europe during the 15th and 16th centuries. It became popular in the Americas with the rise of cattle ranching.

      Tacos history

      Steak tacos, rooted in ancient Mexican tradition, combine grilled beef with corn tortillas. "Tacos de carne asada" became popular in Mexico and the U.S., reflecting the fusion of indigenous and European influences.
